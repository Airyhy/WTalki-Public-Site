Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Overview

WTalkie ("the App") is a voice-powered gym workout tracker. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile photo from your Google or Apple sign-in. Used to identify your account and sync data.
  • Workout data: Exercises, sets, reps, weights, and workout sessions you log. Stored to provide workout history and progress tracking.
  • Profile data: Age, gender, weight, height, and gym preferences you optionally provide. Used to personalize your experience.
  • Voice data: Audio from the microphone is processed for voice commands. Audio is sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription and is not stored permanently.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the App's features
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase
  • To process voice commands via AI services

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: Voice transcription (Whisper) and AI assistant (ChatGPT). OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple authentication. Apple Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) servers. Data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time from Profile → Delete Account.

Your Rights

  • Access your data at any time through the App
  • Delete your account and all cloud data from the Profile tab
  • Contact us to request data export or have questions about your data

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at airyhy@gmail.com.
