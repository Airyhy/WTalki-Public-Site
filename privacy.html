Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

WTalkie ("the App") is a voice-powered gym workout tracker. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email, and profile photo from your Google or Apple sign-in.
  • Workout data: Exercises, sets, reps, weights, and sessions you log.
  • Profile data: Age, gender, weight, height, and gym preferences you optionally provide.
  • Voice data: Audio is processed for voice commands via OpenAI Whisper and is not stored permanently by WTalkie or OpenAI.

AI Features & Data Disclosure

WTalkie uses AI-powered features to help you plan and log workouts. When you use these features, the following data may be sent to third-party AI services:

  • Voice recordings: When you use the voice assistant, audio is sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription. Audio is processed in real time and is not stored permanently by OpenAI or WTalkie.
  • Workout context: To provide personalized suggestions, the AI assistant receives your current workout state, exercise names, recent workout history (up to 30 days), gym information, and basic profile data (age, gender, weight). This data is sent to OpenAI's ChatGPT API.
  • Text input: Any text you type to the AI assistant is sent to OpenAI for processing.

OpenAI's API data usage policy states that data sent via the API is not used to train their models. For details, see OpenAI's API Data Usage Policies.

All AI features require explicit user action (tapping the microphone or sending a message). The app never sends data to AI services in the background or without your knowledge.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the App's features
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase
  • To process voice commands and AI assistant requests via OpenAI
  • To personalize workout suggestions based on your history and preferences

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and cloud storage. Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: Voice transcription (Whisper API) and AI assistant (ChatGPT API). Privacy Policy · API Data Policy
  • Apple: Sign in with Apple. Privacy Policy

Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored on Google Firebase, Google's enterprise-grade cloud platform. Firebase provides industry-standard security including HTTPS encryption in transit, encryption at rest, and compliance with SOC 1/2/3 and ISO 27001 certifications. Your data is protected by the same infrastructure that powers Google's own products.

Voice audio and AI requests are transmitted over encrypted connections and are not stored permanently after processing.

Data Retention & Deletion

Data is retained while your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time directly from the App — go to Profile → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and permanent; all your workout data, profile information, and account credentials are removed from our servers.

Your Rights

  • Access your data anytime through the App
  • Delete your account and all cloud data instantly from the Profile tab — no email required, no waiting period
  • Contact us for data export or questions

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
