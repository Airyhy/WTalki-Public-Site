Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use voice commands?

Tap the sparkles button on the Today tab, then speak or type your command. For example: "Help me design a leg workout" or "Add barbell bench press."

How do I add a new exercise?

Go to Profile → My Movements → tap the + button. You can set the name, category, and tracking type (reps + weight, reps only, or time-based).

Is my data backed up?

Yes. WTalkie syncs your data to the cloud via your Google or Apple account. Your workouts, movements, and profile are automatically saved.

How do I change my weight unit?

Go to Profile → Personal Info. You can toggle between lbs and kg next to the weight field.
