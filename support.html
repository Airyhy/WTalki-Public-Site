How do I use voice commands?

Tap the sparkles button on the Today tab, then speak or type your command. For example: "Help me design a leg workout" or "Add barbell bench press."

How do I add a new exercise?

Go to the Exercises tab and tap the + button in the top right. Set the name, category, and tracking type (reps + weight, reps only, or time-based).

Is my data backed up?

Yes. WTalkie syncs your data to the cloud via your Google or Apple account automatically.

How do I change my weight unit?

Go to the Profile tab → Personal Info section. Use the Unit picker to switch between Pounds (lbs) and Kilograms (kg).

Does WTalkie use AI?

Yes. WTalkie includes an AI assistant that can help design workouts, add exercises, and answer questions about your progress. Voice input is transcribed using OpenAI Whisper, and the assistant uses ChatGPT to understand your requests. All AI actions require your explicit confirmation before any changes are made. See our Privacy Policy for details on how AI data is handled.
