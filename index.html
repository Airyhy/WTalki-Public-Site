How It Works

Like texting a coach who never sleeps.

You speak, it understands

Natural language, not commands. Say "add bench press" or "I'm done for today" — WTalkie gets it.

It confirms before acting

Nothing happens without your OK. WTalkie always tells you what it's about to do and waits for a yes.

Everything is remembered

Every workout, every set, every personal record — synced to the cloud, tracked over time, always there when you need it.
